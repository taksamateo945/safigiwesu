Leah Messer Ends Rehab Stint, Shares Photos of Three Daughters

Completing her time! Teen Mom 2 star Leah Messer wrapped up a stint in rehab on Sunday, June 21, a source confirms to Us Weekly. The 23-year-old reality star had entered a facility due to stress and depression earlier this month. 

During her stay in rehab, her divorce from second husband Jeremy Calvert was finalized, giving Messer custody of the pair's daughter, Adalynn, 2, while Calvert will have visitation rights. Messer is also mom to twins Ali and Aleeah, 5, with her ex Corey Simms.

Hopefully Messer will get some quality time with her daughters before her private life is aired for everyone to see. In the trailer for the new season of Teen Mom 2, the tearful West Virginia native is seen saying, "I've been accused of being an unfit mother, abusing drugs."
