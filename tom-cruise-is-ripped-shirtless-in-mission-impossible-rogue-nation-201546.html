Tom Cruise really hit the gym before reprising his role as Ethan Hunt! The 52-year-old actor looks half his age (those abs!) in the first action-packed trailer for Mission: Impossible – Rogue Nation.

Hunt is back for more crazy death-defying adventures for the franchise’s fifth flick. This time, he and his team battle it out with the international rogue organization Syndicate before they have a chance to take down the IMF.

But before Hunt finds himself hanging from a plane in the sky (seriously, hold on man!), he nearly dies after losing oxygen during a mission, takes charge in an insane car chase, and, um, looks extra handsome at a black-tie event.
