Cheryl stuns at KOBOX launch

Cheryl Fernandez-Versini was looking stunning last night as she attended the official launch of KOBOX, the UK’s first boutique boxing gym.

The workout studio is situated on the posh New King’s road Chelsea in London and has been set up by, Cheryl’s friend and PA Lily England’s boyfriend Shane Collins.

The gorgeous Geordie was looking happy and relaxed, wearing a navy blue coat, ripped jeans and a logo print sweater, as she happily posed for photos on the red carpet in front of the waiting press.

Prior to the evening launch, Cheryl had enjoyed her first boxing session at the KOBOX gym, and shared a post workout selfie on Instagram with the caption: K O’d by Shane @kobox (sic).

For more info on what KOBOX offer and how to book classes at the gym, you can visit their official website.
