COIN Announce Some 2019 Tour Dates

Alternative sweethearts, COIN, have released some dates for their 2019 tour. Chase Lawrence, the band’s lead singer, said on Twitter that this is the band’s “biggest touring year yet.”

2019 will likely be our biggest touring year yet. We're gonna everywhere, but don't miss these love you soooooo much https://t.co/E34axC64fy

— chase (@chase_lawrence) November 27, 2018

The “Paradise of Thought” tour, as noted by the top-left corner of the tour poster, is set to begin on February 5 in Orlando, Florida and will through March 1 in Los Angeles, California — their largest headlining show to date.

Ahhh this is our biggest headlining show yet. Can we see every single one of you????!!!! Tickets on sale Friday 10AM pic.twitter.com/qCOiqiG4ez

— COIN (@COIN) November 13, 2018

Although there hasn’t been anything but subtle hints at their third album, fans have already been surprised with two singles — “Simple Romance” and “Cemetery.”

The two singles, although different from the previous two albums, are natural progressions of COIN’s sound — these are two singles that are still inherently COIN.

The list of dates are as follows:

February 5 — Orlando, FL

February 6 — Atlanta, GA

February 8 — Washington, DC

February 9 — New York, NY

February 10 — Boston, MA

February 12 — Toronto, ON

February 15 — Columbus, OH

February 17 — Dallas, TX

February 20 — Salt Lake City, UT

February 23 — Seattle, WA

February 24 — Vancouver, BC

February 27 — San Francisco, CA

March 1 — Los Angeles, CA

Tickets for all of the dates, excluding the Los Angeles date, go on sale Friday, November 30, 2018. The times vary, but most go on sale at 10 a.m. local time.

And as noted by Ryan Winnen, the band’s drummer extraordinaire, fans can expect more shows to be announced over the next few weeks.
