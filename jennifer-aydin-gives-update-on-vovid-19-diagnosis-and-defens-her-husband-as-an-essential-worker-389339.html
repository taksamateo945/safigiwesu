Jennifer Aydin Gives Update On COVID-19 Diagnosis And Defends Her Husband As An Essential Worker

Real Housewives of New Jersey star Jennifer Aydin revealed that she was diagnosed with COVID-19. After her scary experience, the reality star updated fans on how she’s feeling.

On Apil 8, Jennifer revealed via Instagram that she started experiencing Coronavirus symptoms in late March and isolated herself before she received positive test results.

On April 16, the RHONJ personality revealed she has made a full recovery.

She took to her social media to share a lovely throwback family photo. It was also her birthday.

The snapshot was captioned with: ‘Here’s a throwback that makes me so grateful for what we have- #family We were celebrating my birthday with some of the most important people in my life! So- we can be still in this moment in time an appreciation all the little things. I celebrate this birthday with just Thanking God for my health, friends and family. And if all else fails, we can pretend that this year doesn’t count! #stayyoung PS- I feel great! Amazing! No symptoms since Sunday and I feel better than ever! #oneyearolder #thisyeardoesntcount! #itsmybirthday #tbt.’

Meanwhile, people have questioned why her husband is still working since he is a plastic surgeon. Some followers didn’t believe that the type of doctor he is was essential since it has been advised to cancel all elective surgeries to save room for COVID patients.

Earlier this month Aydin shared a video of her husband Bill preparing to go into emergency surgery for a patient who attempted suicide.

Jennifer explained: ‘And people wonder why a plastic surgeon is still working or how he has access to tests. It’s not all t*ts and a*s people!’

She was not the only Bravolebrity who contracted the horrible respiratory illness.

Mr. Bravo himself (aka Andy Cohen) shared his diagnosis and also made a full recovery.
