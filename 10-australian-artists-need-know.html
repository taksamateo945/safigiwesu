10 Australian artists you need to know about

Australia might not have a large population but it does have an enormous pool of talent. Home of artists like queen Kylie, Sia or super producer Flume, Aussies definitely know how to compose great songs.

Several local artists have lately been making waves online and on Australian radio, and now they are ready to shine worldwide.

We present you: ‘10 Australian artists on their way to conquer the world’

Starley

Pop, singer-songwriter Starley has quickly escalated the streaming charts, currently at number 13 on Spotify Global and 23 on Shazam. Her ‘Call on Me’ single was a big hit in Australia and its remix by Ryan Riback has shot her into popstar land.

William Singe

Will became popular with his smooth auditions on The X Factor Australia in 2012. After different projects, he decided to start posting r&b and hip-hop covers on his FB channel in 2014, which became viral shortly afterwards. Now with almost 3 million followers on Facebook and a huge YouTube channel, we can’t wait for his debut solo work coming out this same month.

Tkay Maidza

Singer and rapper Tkay Maidza has been already nominated for a Best New International Act BET Award. Having collaborated with the likes of Martin Solveig and fellow Australian Troye Sivan, only good things can come from her.

Pon Cho & Paige IV

There are times when great creatives get together and come out with amazing songs. Producer Pon Cho and singer Paige IV have formed a collaborative duo we hope keep creating together for many years to come.

Mallrat

Australian girls have a special talent when it comes to creating catchy pop songs. Mallrat is also that type of girl you want to be friends with. Think of Alessia Cara going Charli XCX. Mallrat is coming out with great party bangers, so don’t miss out.

Woodes

Alternative singer-songwriter, Elle Graham´s (Woodes) voice will hook you forever. Recently signed to Universal, you can sit and relax for hours listening to her lovely songs.

Amy Shark

Creator of one of the catchiest songs of the last months, Amy Shark is starting to make her mark out of Australia. Check out Amy’s YouTube channel, you will surely ‘Adore’ her.

Tash Sultana

One of the coolest girls you’ll meet this year. Tash Sultana’s music is perfect to chill and forget about everything. Just waiting for her music to be played in every summer party.

Cosmo’s Midnight

Electronic duo, Cosmo’s Midnight show us the talent Australian young producers have. Their latest track History is on its way to become a floor filler.

Cassian

Cassian is a DJ and producer that has already played at Coachella and travelled half of the world. If you are a fan of electronic music just check him out, you will love his tracks.
