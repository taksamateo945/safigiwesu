Is Jamie Foxx Married? Who is His Wife or Ex-Wife?

Jamie Foxx is not married; however, he’s been in three failed relationships with Katie Holmes, Connie Kline, and Kristin Grannis.

Additionally, Jamie has two daughters: Corrine Foxx, and Annalise Bishop from his previous relationships. Despite being in several relationships, the Oscar Award winner has never been married and doesn’t seem to have plans to get married anytime soon. Similarly, the veteran actor has never proposed to any of his girlfriends before.

Are Jamie Foxx And Katie Holmes Still Together?

Jamie Foxx and Katie Holmes were together in a romantic relationship from 2013 through 2019. Katie Holmes (born  December 18, 1978, in Toledo, OH), started dating Jamie in May 2013, in a relationship that was kept private and from media attention.

They would, however, eventually break up in 2019 after six years together. The reason for their shocking split is still unknown to the public. However, news of their split came only days after the Just Mercy star was spotted with singer Sela Vave. At the time of their breakup, an insider exclusively disclosed to US Weekly that Katie was the one to pull the plug on their relationship.

Before Jamie, Holmes was married to American superstar actor, Tom Cruise. However, Katie and Cruise got divorced after spending seven years together in marriage. Holmes, meanwhile, has moved on and is now dating American musician Bobby Wooten III. In April 2022, the pair were seen kissing and holding hands during a New York City outing. Bobby was also captured hugging Katie’s mother, Kathy Holmes, at a fun day in Central Park.

How Many Baby Mamas Does Jamie Foxx Have?

Jamie Foxx has two baby mamas, namely Kristin Grannis, and Connie Kline. Both have produced two wonderful daughters named Corinne Foxx and Annalise Bishop for him.

Corinne Foxx, Jamie’s first daughter, was birthed in 1994 by Kristen Grannis. Corinne, who is seemingly following in her father’s footsteps, has won an Emmy for outstanding variety special for her roles in ABC’s “Live in Front of a Studio Audience: All in the Family” and “Good Times.” She’s also recognized for her role as Sasha in “47 Meters Down Uncaged”, in the series “Dollface.”

Since 2018, she’s been appearing alongside her father on the game show, “Beat Shazam,” as the DJ. The young talented actress has a strong bond with her father, even though he is no longer in love with her mother.

On the other hand, Jamie Foxx’s second daughter, Annalise Bishop, who was born in 2006 hasn’t started her professional career yet. Notwithstanding, she’s appeared in several red-carpet events alongside her dad, such as the 2015 Grammys and the 2021 SAG Awards. Like her stepsister, Annalise has a good relationship with her father, Jamie Foxx.
