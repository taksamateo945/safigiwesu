Britney Spears' Former Assistant Felicia Culotta Shares Thoughts on Her Boyfriend Sam Asghari

Britney Spears‘ former assistant Felicia Culotta, who is known for her nickname “Fe,” is opening up about the singer’s current boyfriend Sam Asghari.

If you watched the new documentary Framing Britney Spears, then you’ve seen Felicia speak out about Britney and her conservatorship.

Fe started working with Britney right at the beginning of her career and she is still a part of her life after all of these years.

In an interview with Us Weekly, Fe shared her thoughts on Sam.

Click inside to find out what she said…

“I know Sam. He is a wonderful guy,” Felicia said. “Not only is he extremely well-spoken and kind, he is simply and utterly so handsome, and they look great together!”

When asked about the documentary, Felicia said, “I am most happy that the New York Times doc asked me to give a bit of the ‘beginning’ of my time with Britney because I truly knew the fans fell head over heels with her. I just wanted the world to remember that.”
