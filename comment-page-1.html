Lauren London is an American actress, model, and television personality. Her roles include the films ATL, This Christmas, Next Day Air, Madea’s Big Happy Family, and Without Remorse, and the show The Game.

Her father, Gary, who is caucasian, is Ashkenazi Jewish. Her mother, Terri, is African-American. A picture of her parents can be seen here.

Lauren has a son with her former partner, rapper Lil Wayne; and a son with her partner, late rapper and songwriter Nipsey Hussle.

Lauren’s maternal grandfather’s surname was Conner.

Lauren’s maternal grandmother’s surname was Crosby.
