Kirstie Alley Shows Off Her Noticeably Slimmer Figure See Her Weight Loss!

Kirstie Alley is a glam girl on the go!

The actress, 63, has been keeping a low profile the last few months, but the former ‘Cheers’ star was spotted at LAX Airport on Wednesday, Dec. 10 — and she looks amazing!

Kirstie showed off her noticeable weight loss in a chic emerald green coat with navy blue leggings and matching embellished flats.

Kirstie at LAX on Dec. 10, 2014.

Kirstie — who has always chosen to be very open about her body woes — announced her renewed partnership with weight loss company Jenny Craig back in April.

“We welcomed her with open arms,” Jenny Craig CEO Monty Sharma said last spring of Kirstie’s return. “We hope that having Kirstie return as our ambassador will provide continued encouragement to our members who have also struggled with their own weight and are looking for a program that works.”

Kirstie in February 2009.

The ‘Dancing with the Stars’ alum first teamed up with Jenny Craig in 2004 and successfully shed 75 pounds with their food and fitness programs before parting ways with the company in 2007.
